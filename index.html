Tomorrow's Another Day

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: this app collects nothing.
Your data never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Tomorrow's Another Day does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no remote databases.

Where your data lives

All mood check-ins, journal entries, contacts, and settings are stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This data is private to your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third parties

We do not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that transmit data. The app works entirely offline.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device using iOS's built-in notification system. No message content is sent to any external server.

Biometrics

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID to lock the app, biometric data is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave. We never see or store it.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Because we collect no data, any changes are unlikely to affect you.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@darkslab.app

Medical disclaimer

Tomorrow's Another Day is a personal mood tracking tool. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline.
